1- Re:Zero -Starting Life in Another World- (Light Novel)

This story follows Subaru Natsuki, an ordinary teenager who is suddenly transported to a fantasy world . He soon discovers he has a unique and painful power: "Return by Death," which sends him back in time to a specific point whenever he is killed . Subaru must use this ability to save the people he grows to care about, particularly a silver-haired half-elf named Emilia, from deadly situations, enduring immense psychological trauma as he experiences death repeatedly to find the one path that leads to a positive outcome . The series is known for its psychological thriller elements, deconstructing the isekai genre by focusing on the protagonist's suffering and growth rather than granting him overwhelming power.

"I love you... even just saying it doesn't convey the feeling. I want you to stay by my side. I want to always be with you. I want to think about you, to look at you, to touch you, to possess you, and to have you possess me. You are my life. Everything about you. 2- Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation (Light Novel)

The series begins with a 34-year-old unnamed, reclusive NEET who dies after saving a group of teenagers from a traffic accident . He is reincarnated in a world of swords and sorcery as a baby named Rudeus Greyrat, retaining all the memories of his past life . Determined to live this new life to the fullest without regrets, he applies the lessons from his previous failures to become a powerful mage, studying under the demon Roxy, befriending the timid Sylphiette, and later tutoring the violent noble girl Eris . The story chronicles Rudeus's entire life, from childhood to adulthood, as he goes on adventures, forms deep relationships, starts a family, and confronts major threats, earning its reputation as a foundational work that popularized many modern isekai tropes

"This second life is different from the first. I won't waste it. I will live it without regrets."

— Rudeus Greyrat

3- One Piece (Manga)

This epic adventure follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after accidentally eating a Devil Fruit . Inspired by his idol, the pirate "Red-Haired" Shanks, Luffy sets out from his village at age 17 to find the legendary treasure known as "One Piece" and become the King of the Pirates . To achieve his goal, he assembles a diverse and loyal crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, including a swordsman, navigator, sniper, chef, and doctor, each with their own dreams . Their journey takes them across treacherous seas, where they befriend quirky islands, challenge corrupt world powers, and uncover the lost history of the world, all while Luffy's unwavering spirit and loyalty to his friends help him overcome increasingly powerful foes

"I don't want to conquer anything. I just think that the man with the most freedom in the world is the Pirate King!"

— Monkey D. Luffy
